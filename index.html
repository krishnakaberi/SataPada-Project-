Main Attraction:

Chilika Lake is the main attraction here. You can enjoy boating and watching dolphins in the lake, especially the Irrawaddy dolphins, which are rare and found in few places in the world.

About satapada

What is Satapada famous for?

Satapada is primarily famous for dolphin watching, particularly for the Irrawaddy dolphins in Chilika Lake. It's also known as a gateway to Chilika Lake and offers boat rides for tourists to spot these dolphins. Additionally, Satapada is recognized for the Sea Mouth Island where the Chilika Lake meets the Bay of Bengal.

🌊 Satapada Overview

Location: Satapada is in the Puri district of Odisha, India.

It is located on the southern part of Chilika Lake, the largest brackish water lagoon in Asia.

🚤 Activities at Satapada

Dolphin Watching, Boating and Canoeing, Bird Watching, Visit Nalabana Bird Sanctuary (optional, seasonal), Photography of nature and sunset

October to March – Pleasant weather and good time for dolphin and bird watching.
